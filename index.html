FAQ

Common Questions

Can I get a website in my own language?
Absolutely. I offer multilingual setups in English, Spanish, and Italian — perfect for businesses serving multiple European markets.
How fast can you deliver?
Tier 1 projects launch in 3–5 business days. Tier 2 typically takes 1–2 weeks, and Tier 3 projects run 2–4 weeks depending on complexity.
What if I need something not listed in the packages?
No problem at all. The packages cover the most common needs, but I regularly take on fully custom projects — from complex integrations to bespoke platforms.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. Every project includes post-launch support, and I offer ongoing maintenance retainers for continuous updates and performance monitoring.
What's the payment structure?
Typically 50% upfront to begin work, and 50% upon launch. For larger custom projects, we can agree on milestone-based payments.
Which countries do you serve?
I primarily work with clients in the United Kingdom, Italy, and Spain — but I'm happy to take on projects from anywhere in Europe and beyond.
